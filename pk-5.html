DEFENDING LAWSUITS

Being named in a lawsuit or legal action as a defendant, sometimes through no fault of your own, is often stressful and requires immediate action. Consequences can seem daunting and perceptions about what the facts in the case are often in significant dispute. Legal defense of both business and civil matters is a serious issue that must be attended to expeditiously. ACE Law is here to assist you in navigating the defense of your case. From the response to the complaint, discovery and depositions, responding to motions and filings, negotiations and resolutions, to appearing before courts for hearing, among other things, ACE Law will be there to represent you and support you. We will help you resolve disputes through negotiation, mediation, or trial in federal, state or appellate courts. We evaluate the case and provide you with clear understanding of your legal options and provide you with advice as to the most effective and efficient manner of handling the case as to collaborate to make the best decision for your business.

FILING A LAWSUIT?

Sometimes, notwithstanding the parties’ best attempts to resolve their disputes amicably, filing a lawsuit is the only solution. This can arise in a variety of scenarios, both personal and commercial, in which the only way to obtain justice is through the judicial system. The breadth of underlying disagreements or disputes that can lead to this resort are enumerable. If you are involved in a commercial dispute, ACE Law can provide you with the knowledge and skill needed to ensure your rights are protected. ACE Law has extensive experience working with individuals and businesses of all sizes that are looking for a reliable and efficient way to address their legal matters. Common claims that ACE Law firm has assisted clients defend include breach of contract, fraud, tortious interference with contractual relationships, shareholder and partnership disputes, breach of fiduciary duty, negligence and pre- and post-judgement remedies, such as injunctive relief ACE Law is well equipped to assist you with the filing and prosecution of your case. We have extensive experience helping individuals and business owners file legal actions and will support you throughout the process. From advising you on whether a lawsuit is necessary or appropriate for achieving your goals, developing a strategy to achieve a quick and efficient resolution, through trial if necessary, we will be there to assist you and guide you.
